Favourite Places

TAJ MAHAL

The Taj Mahal, a stunning white marble mausoleum in Agra, India, was built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. Renowned for its breathtaking beauty and intricate design, it is a symbol of eternal love and one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

GOLDEN TEMPLE

The Golden Temple, located in Amritsar, India, is the holiest shrine of Sikhism, known for its golden facade and serene surroundings. It symbolizes spiritual peace and equality, welcoming visitors from all faiths with its free community kitchen, serving thousands daily.

CHARMINAR

The Charminar, an iconic monument in Hyderabad, India, was built in 1591 by Sultan Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah. Nearby, the Bhagyalakshmi Temple is situated at the base of Charminar, adding religious significance to the historic structure.
